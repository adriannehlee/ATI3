Making an online Access to information request

Overview of request process

You will be directed through a series of steps to provide the details of your request:

  1. Search prior completed requests to see if the government has already released the information you want to request.
  2. Select the institution you wish to ask for information.
  3. Login, register or proceed as guest.
  4. Provide request details. You will also have the ability to provide any supporting documentation that could help the institution process your request.
  5. Provide contact information.
  6. Review request details.
  7. Pay $5 processing fee: You will be directed to the government's trusted and secure payment partner, Moneris Solutions, to provide your credit card information to pay the $5 processing fee.
  8. Once your payment is processed, you will return to this system and see a confirmation message.
  9. An email will be sent to you at the email address you verified earlier in the process, containing a summary of your request.
  10. You will then have the option of completing a short feedback questionnaire, exiting the system or submitting a new request.
