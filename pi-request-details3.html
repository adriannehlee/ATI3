Information about deceased individuals

Information about deceased individuals can be released if the individual has been dead for more than 20 years.

If the individual has been deceased for fewer than 20 years, only the executor of the estate or one having Power of Attorney may request the deceased's personal information to administer the estate of that person, but only for the purpose of such administration.
