Attach documents

You have requested personal information about another individual. In order to process this request, please provide Proof of Consent documentation.

  • The consent must indicate the name of the person giving his or her consent and to whom consent is being given. The consent document must be dated and signed by the person giving the consent.
  • You must include the consent for each individual listed in your file over the age of 18.
  • Files can be DOC, DOCX, PDF, TIFF, JPG, or PNG.
  • Each file must be less than 2 Mb.
  • The total size of all files must be less than 5 Mb.
