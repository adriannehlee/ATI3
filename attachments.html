Attach documents (optional)

To help clarify your request, you have the options of including supporting files. Please only include documents that complement your request such as consent form, proof of right of access, etc.

  • Attach up to five (5) documents.
  • Files can be DOC, DOCX, PDF, TIFF, JPG, or PNG.
  • Each file must be less than 2 Mb.
  • The total size of all files must be less than 5 Mb.
